  • No pretreatment & maintenance free

    The traditional need-to-be-polished electrode want a time-consuming polishing process, or even a complex pretreatment process.Screen printed and Interdigitated electrodes provide you with a "Ready to be Used" solution to effectively save your experiment time.

  • Excellent electrochemical performance & Small lot-to-lot difference

    Zensor's screen printed electrodes are manufactured by our exquisite production technolog ,which batch difference is less than 5% and can be used continuously for more than 24h *. The most important thing is that the electrochemical performance is equally excellent, providing you with the disposable and lab-analysis-level sensor.

    * In a specific solution and experimental environment.

    The images above are CVs of GC,SPE, Interdigitated electrodes in 3mM Ferricyanide with 0.1 M KCl,pH=7

  • Why using Screen printed electrodes(SPE) can accelerate research?

    Just need a few microliters of sample

    Screen printed electrodes just require a few microliters to complete the test. SPE are a perfect solution for electrochemical tests on precious and expensive samples

    Suitable for the in-situ tests

    SPE can be used in conjunction with IR,UV,Raman spectrum, NMR, TEM, microscopy and other detection equipments
    and other detection equipments

  • With the Electrochemical Flow Cell, easy for real-time testing

    Image:SPE / EFC combined with HPLC apply in Real-time testing

  • Effectively shorten the experiment time and make the data more reliable

    Screen printed electrodes are designed to be disposable, so you do not have to worry about electrode pretreatment and maintenance procedures.You can used SPE as you like and don't worry about electrode broken.In addition, in the same experimental environment, multiple electrodes can be modified and tested at the same time.That can complete repeatability tests within a short time ,and get more reliable data

If you are a first-time to use SPE, you may consider our regular models first.
