Multi-Channel Potentiostat/Galvanostat

MCP100

  • 2 to 10 channels design
  • simultaneous or individual mode available
  • 3 methods of electrochemical experiment

  • 2 to 10 channels design

    2 Basic channels, expandable to 10 maximum channels

  • Coordinated & Separated mode available

    Simultaneous Mode： Multiple electrodes can be tested together under the same experimental conditions to ensure the reliability and reproducibility of experimental data.

    Individual Mode： Each channel can perform different detection methods on the same sample and more experimental data can be obtained faster.

  • 3 methods of electrochemical test

    • CV (Cyclic Voltammetry)

    • IT (Amperometry)

    • OCP (Open-Circuit Potential)

Main Function (A)Multi-channels with individual control of 3 electrodes system.
(B)Multi-channels of electrochemical graphs (including CV,IT,OCP) showing simultaneously or individually.
(C)Execution of electrochemaical methods continuously and automatically.
(D)Save data automatically.
(E)Data Analysis automatically.
Electrochemical Method (A)Cyclic Voltammetry (CV)
(B)Amperometry (IT)
(C)Open circuit potential (OCP)
Range (A)Potentail range: +2~-2V(V)
(B)Measured current range: 10-2~10-10
(C)Channels:2~10
Others (A)Potential resolution: 1mV
(B)Potential accuracy: +- 0.1%
(C)Measured currrent resolution: 0.006% current range (1.2fA on 10pA)
(D)Sampling rate: 1ms
(E)Power requirement: AC100~250V,50~60Hz,40W
Size 230(L)*370(W)*130(H)mm
